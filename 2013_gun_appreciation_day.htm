2013 Gun Appreciation Day – High Capacity Steel Fun Shoot – January 19th 2013, 9:30 AM
There will be three different ‘stages’ for your shooting enjoyment!
    1. We will have a ‘Man-on-Man’ steel shoot. This will be single elimination. This will be centerfire pistol only, 9mm and above. There will be a number of steel falling targets, and a ‘crossover popper’ which will determine the winner of each pairing. Entry fee $5. We may even do it more than once, if time allows. Remember, this is for fun!
    2. High Capacity Steel Challenge Stage. This will be like a regular steel stage, but there will be 12 or so targets! (hence the high capacity....) These are not reactive targets, so we will have divisions for centerfire, and rimfire. All centerfire will be lumped together. All rimfire (rifle and pistol) will be lumped together. Only two divisions – centerfire and rimfire. period. Remember, this is for fun. Entry Fee – $2 per (2) runs, best time counts. We will keep running people until we get tired, or just can’t take it anymore! We will run our Big Display Board timer, so everyone can keep an eye on what the times are, even if they are hiding in the warm clubhouse!
    3. .223 Rifle Steel speed stage – AR15’s are on everybody’s mind right now, so we thought we would make a place for them. We will setup a handful of steel targets out to 100 yards, which will be engaged with .223 rifle ONLY. Entry Fee – $2 per (2) runs, best time counts. Did I mention – this is for FUN!
Notes:
  • All start positions will be LOW READY, no holster draws. Works better with winter coats.
  • Yes, all different guns are lumped together, other than rimfire on stage 2 – why? This is just for fun!!
  • PLEASE bring a friend, they will have fun!
  • What are we going to do with the money? We are going to have a list of a few organizations that the we will choose from to send the money. We will take a vote, each participant gets one vote. List to be determined...
  • If you want to bring some food along to share with the group – please feel free! I don’t care what it is, as long as you want to bring it!
  • don’t have a .223 rifle??? Hmmmm..... bring some factory ammo, and I am certain you will find one that someone will loan you to shoot!
  • What do the winners get? Drum roll please......BRAGGING RIGHTS!
  • We will try to have some tarps on hand to help catch brass. If you have a spare, bring it along!
