Magic City Action Shooters is a USPSA competitive shooting club, based at the Billings Rod and Gun Club, in Billings, MT.

We welcome new competitors! - just bring your handgun (9mm or larger) 150 rounds of ammo, a holster, and some spare magazines (or speedloaders), eye and ear protection, and we'll show you the rest!

Visit the USPSA website for more new competitor information, here is some good information from the Area 1 website also. 

MORE INFO: If you'd like to receive emails of matches and other club news you can sign up for our mailing list. If you have any other questions please send an e-mail to: contact@magiccityactionshooters.org
